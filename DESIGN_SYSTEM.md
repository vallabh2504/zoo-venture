# DESIGN_SYSTEM.md - Juicy Skeuomorphism & Depth

## Philosophy: "Digital Toy"
The UI should feel like a premium toy. Chunky, colorful, and tactile.

## Color Palette: "Safari Sunset"
- **Primary:** `bg-orange-500` (Sunset)
- **Secondary:** `bg-green-600` (Jungle Leaf)
- **Accent:** `bg-yellow-400` (Sun/Banana)
- **Surface:** `bg-cream-100` (Paper/Canvas texture)

## Typography
- **Headings:** Rounded, bold sans-serif (e.g., Nunito, Poppins, or system rounded).
- **Body:** Readable, high contrast.

## Card Styles: "Squishy 3D"
- **Border:** Thick, colored borders (4px-6px).
- **Shadow:** Hard shadows (no blur) or deep soft shadows depending on "toy" vibe.
  - *Decision:* Hard shadow offset (4px 4px 0px rgba(0,0,0,0.2)) for retro/toy feel.
- **Corner Radius:** Large (rounded-xl or rounded-2xl).
- **Texture:** Subtle noise or SVG patterns (dots/stripes) on card backgrounds.

## Interactive Elements
- **Buttons:** Pill-shaped, behave like physical buttons (press down).
- **Progress:** Juicy, liquid-like filling animations.

## Background: "Living World"
- **Layer 0:** Gradient Sky.
- **Layer 1:** Floating particles (Fireflies).
- **Layer 2:** Foreground UI (Cards).
